About Us

Sea Seven Shipping LLP

At Sea Seven Shipping LLP, we are a vibrant and dynamic team of professionals with approximately 15 years of collective experience in the shipping industry. Our expertise spans administrative, commercial, and operational management of fleets, offering brokering services, single-window, and end-to-end solutions for both main fleet and offshore operations, as well as a variety of port projects. With an extensive network across shipyards, shipowners, and key players in the oil, gas, and offshore industries, we ensure reliable and efficient service delivery.

We provide cost-effective, customized solutions for ship management, port operations, standardized ship stores, ship support services, repairs, and liaising with port authorities and CHAs. We also specialize in the hiring of spud pontoons, barges, tugs, crew boats, dredgers, and offering logistics support, including fast rescue boat hire.
