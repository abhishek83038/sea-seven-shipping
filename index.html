About Us

JSS SEA SHIP MARINE SERVICE LLP

At JSS SEA SHIP MARINE SERVICE LLP, we are a vibrant and dynamic team of professionals with approximately 15 years of collective experience in the shipping industry. Our expertise spans administrative, commercial, and operational management of fleets, offering single-window and end-to-end solutions for both main fleet and offshore operations.

there is two director NITESH YADAV AND JAGRITI YADAV, About our director *

NITESH YADAV - Director

Mr. Nitesh Yadav is a dedicated maritime professional with a strong background in ship management and crew operations. With several years of experience in the marine sector, he has worked closely with reputed shipping agencies and understands the complete process of crew recruitment, documentation, and compliance with DG Shipping norms.

He has a keen focus on safety, training, and professional growth of seafarers. Under his leadership, the company ensures that every crew member is deployed as per STCW and MLC guidelines.

His vision is to create a transparent and reliable platform for Indian seafarers to explore global opportunities while maintaining international maritime standards.

Core Expertise:
  • Crew Management & Documentation
  • DG Shipping Compliance
  • STCW/MLC Regulations
  • Marine Safety & Training Coordination
Ms. Jagriti Yadav — Director

Ms. Jagriti Yadav plays a key role in administration, compliance, and human resource management. She brings strong organizational and leadership skills to ensure smooth day-to-day functioning of the company.

With a deep understanding of maritime operations and recruitment systems, she oversees company policy implementation, crew welfare, and quality management in line with ISO and RPSL requirements.

Her approach focuses on ethical recruitment practices, transparency, and long-term growth for both seafarers and clients.

Core Expertise:
  • HR & Administrative Operations
  • ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management
  • Seafarer Welfare & Documentation
  • Client Coordination & Office Compliance
