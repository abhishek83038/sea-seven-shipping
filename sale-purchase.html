About Our Sale & Purchase

our Sale & Purchase (S&P) division provides trusted, end-to-end solutions for clients looking to acquire, sell, or invest in vessels across global shipping markets. With decades of combined expertise and a vast international network of ship owners, charterers, and brokers, we ensure every transaction is handled with transparency, efficiency, and strategic insight.

We understand that ship acquisition or sale is not just a transaction—it’s a long-term investment. That’s why we guide our clients through each stage of the process, ensuring smooth negotiations, accurate documentation, and regulatory compliance at every step.

Our Sale & Purchase services include:

Our Sale & Purchase services include:

New & Second-Hand Vessel Acquisition – Sourcing and securing vessels tailored to specific cargo, trade, and operational requirements.

Vessel Sale Facilitation – Managing the entire process of marketing, negotiation, and closing deals for ship owners looking to sell their assets.

Market Research & Intelligence – Providing up-to-date insights on freight markets, vessel valuations, and investment opportunities.

Valuation & Financial Advisory – Delivering fair market valuations and customized financial strategies to support informed decision-making.

Negotiation & Documentation – Ensuring transparent contract discussions and flawless handling of charter parties, MOAs (Memorandum of Agreement), and delivery protocols.

Inspection & Due Diligence – Conducting technical evaluations, class record checks, and compliance audits to minimize risk before purchase or sale.
